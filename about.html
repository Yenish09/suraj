About Me

Hello! I am Suraj Shahu, a passionate music producer and web developer with a strong background in music recording technologies, guitar, piano, HTML, and CSS. I have worked on various projects that involve co-producing with a band named B-8, where I've contributed to the creation of unique sounds and helped shape the overall musical direction of our tracks.

My journey in music began with a strong interest in playing the guitar. This passion quickly expanded into other areas such as piano and music production. Over the years, I have honed my skills through hands-on experience and self-learning, diving deep into the technical aspects of sound engineering and music production. This journey has allowed me to blend creativity with technical expertise, resulting in a versatile skill set that spans both music and web development.

When I'm not working on projects, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading books on music theory, or hiking in nature to find inspiration. I'm also a big fan of experimenting with different genres of music and constantly seek out new sounds to incorporate into my productions. I'm always eager to learn new things and take on challenges that help me grow as a professional, whether in the studio or coding new web applications.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch if you'd like to collaborate, share ideas, or learn more about my work. I'm always open to new opportunities and love connecting with others who share my passion for music and technology.
